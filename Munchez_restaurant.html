Our Story

More than a restaurant — a destination

Muncheez Bistro is Nairobi's best kept secret, hidden inside Total Petrol Station along Northern Bypass Road in Membley. What started as a bold culinary vision has grown into one of the area's most cherished dining spots.

Our chef sources the finest ingredients — fresh seafood, premium cuts, aromatic spices — crafting dishes that leave a lasting impression. From garlic butter jumbo prawns to honey glazed pork ribs, every plate is a work of art served beautifully on wooden boards.

Beyond the food, Muncheez is a lifestyle. Slow jams set the mood, board games bring the laughter, and our team makes sure every guest — from solo diners to birthday parties — leaves with a smile.
